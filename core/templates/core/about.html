About getJustice

getJustice combines social media and social activism to allow users to report cases of injustice they see online to civil rights organizations and their local representatives quickly and securely. Our goal is to increase police accountability, decrease the length of time it takes for an instance of injustice to be seen on social media before a suspect is charged with a crime, and foster a new generation of political activists by providing millenials with easy access to activate their political power.

Disclaimer:

Any reports to any government entities made using getJustice are the responsibility of the user who created them and are in no way associated with getJustice. Users who file false reports will be removed from the service. We can not guarantee that users will receive a response in regards to their report although responses are encouraged. If you are in an emergency situation please contact 911.
