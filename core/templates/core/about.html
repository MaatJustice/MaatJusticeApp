MaatJustice combines social media and social activism to allow users to report cases of injustice they see online to the police and local FBI field offices. Our goal is to increase police accountability, repair officer relations with their communities, and decrease the length of time it takes for an instance of injustice to be seen on social media before a suspect is charged with a crime.
